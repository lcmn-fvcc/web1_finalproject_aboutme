Books

I love books.

I love to read, but I also love to collect books faster than I can read them. I collect books that I either want to read or have read and loved and want to keep forever. Just seeing the covers reminds me of the stories or information they contain. I love to find used books at used book stores or thrift stores. Can't pass up a good deal on a book!

I wouldn't say I stick strictly to any particular genre, but I do tend to love fantasy, sci fi, dystopian novels, and historical fiction. I mostly read fiction for pleasure, but every once in a while I like a good non-fiction book.

Here are just a few of my favorite books of all time:

  • The Power of One by Bryce Courtenay
  • Franny and Zooey by J.D. Salinger
  • The Golden Compass Trilogy by Philip Pullmam (the trilogy is actually called His Dark Materials, but I like the book titles better -- The Golden Compass, The Subtle Knife, The Amber Spyglass)
  • Born to Run by Christopher McDougall
  • The Omnivore's Dilemma by Michael Pollan
  • The Lord of the Rings Trilogy by J.R. Tolkien
  • Prodigal Summer by Barbara Kingsolver
  • The Fountainhead by Ayn Rand
  • The Circle by Dave Eggers
  • Moloka'i by Alan Brennert
  • The Night Circus by Erin Morgenstern
  • Tales of a Female Nomad by Rita Golden Gelman
  • Your Money or Your Life by Joe Dominguez and Vicki Robin
  • The Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins
  • The Runaway Jury by John Grisham
  • Daring Greatly by Brené Brown
  • The Invisible Library by Genevieve Cogman

... Alright, I'll stop now...
