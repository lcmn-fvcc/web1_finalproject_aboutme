Travel

I love to travel. I first started to travel right after high school, when a friend and I took a 3-week trip to Europe, riding the train throughout several countries.

I have since traveled on six of the seven continents and been to 17 different countries outside of the United States. Most recently (besides a quick jaunt to Canada), I traveled to Switzerland in January of 2019. I think it was possibly the best trip I've taken so far. I was also able to visit the tiny country of Leichtenstein, which borders Switzerland and is the last monarchy in the Alps, ruled by Prince Hans-Adam II.

Here is a list of the countries to which I have traveled (not in order of travel):

  1. Argentina
  2. Bahamas
  3. Brazil
  4. Canada
  5. China
  6. Egypt
  7. England
  8. France
  9. Germany
  10. Ireland
  11. Italy
  12. Jordan
  13. Liechtenstein
  14. Mexico
  15. The Netherlands
  16. New Zealand
  17. Switzerland
